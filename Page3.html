Tres Leches Flan Churros

Tres Leches Cake

Servings:4 Serving(s)

Prep Time:10 min

Total Time:20 min

For Dought

Ingredients Size
Water 1 Cup
Sugar 2 1/2 Tbsp
Salt 1/2 Tsp
Vegetable Oil 2 Tbsp
Flour 1 Cup

For the preparation

Ingredients Size
Oil 2 Quarts
Sugar 1/2 Cup
Cinamon 1 tsp
Directions

Combine water, 2 Â½ tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Bring to a boil and remove from the heat. Stir in flour, stirring until mixture forms a ball.


Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or deep pot to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).


Transfer dough to a sturdy pastry bag fitted with a medium star tip. Carefully pipe a few 5- to 6-inch strips of dough into the hot oil; work in batches so you don't crowd the fryer. Cook until golden; use a spider or slotted spoon to transfer churros to paper towels to drain.


Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon. Roll drained churros in cinnamon and sugar mixture.
