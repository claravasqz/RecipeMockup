Tres Leches Flan Churros

Tres Leches Cake

Servings:10 Serving(s)

Prep Time:15 min

Total Time:45 min

For the cake

Ingredients Size
Eggs 9 Units
Sugar 1 Cup
Vanilla Extract 1 Tbsp
Flour 2 Cup

For the milk

Ingredients Size
Condense Milk 1 Can
Evaporate Milk 1 Can
Whole Milk 1 Cup

For the whipped cream

Ingredients Size
Whipped cream 2 Cup
Powdered sugar 1/4 Cup
Directions

Preheat the oven to 175 C. Grease a 23x33 cm mold, and place wax paper cut to the size of the mold on the bottom.


Beat the egg whites in the mixer on medium speed for about 4 to 5 minutes, or until soft peaks form. Slowly add the sugar and continue beating until stiffer peaks form. Turn off the mixer and with a spatula, transfer the egg whites to a large bowl.


Rinse the mixer and now add the yolks and beat at medium speed for about 5 or 6 minutes, or until the yolks are creamy and fluffy and their color is lighter, almost cream-colored. Add the vanilla and continue beating the mixture for 1 more minute.


Pour the yolk mixture into the egg white mixture, and using the spatula, combine both with circular movements until the mixture is homogeneous. Do it carefully so as not to lose too much of the volume of the beaten egg whites. Finally, add the flour with the spatula, passing it along the inside of the mold to make sure there is no flour left unstirred.


Pour the batter into the greased cake pan and bake for 22 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center of the cake. The cake may still be a little moist, but not raw and will have a golden color.


Once cool, transfer the cake to a plate. Remove the wax paper from the bottom, and turn it over. The plate should have enough space for both the cake and the tres leches sauce that you are going to prepare. Use a fork to poke holes all over the cake so it can absorb the sauce well.


Combine the condensed milk, evaporated milk, normal milk and vanilla in a container. Pour this mixture over the entire cake. It may seem like a lot of sauce, but the cake will absorb it all.


In the mixer, beat the cream with the powdered sugar on medium speed until stiff peaks form. Cover the cake with the cream and refrigerate. You can decorate it with red berries or with the ingredients of your liking.
